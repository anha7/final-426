Kitty Courier!

How to Play:

  • You are controlling Bridget the kitty, and you deliver packages across this little town!
  • Use WASD to move and Shift to sprint.
  • A red arrow will point you towards a package you have to collect or a destination you must deliver the package.
  • You pick up packages and deliver them by simply getting within the radius of the point of interest (POI).
  • Have fun, and do not get hit by a car!
